Everything you need, nothing you don't.

TodoPin is designed to be simple, fast, and stay out of your way.

Frameless Windows

No title bars, no distractions. Just your todos floating cleanly on the desktop.

Pin Anywhere

Drag cards to any position, any monitor. They stay exactly where you put them.

Beautiful Themes

7 card colors and 6 app themes including GitHub Dark, Nord, and Dracula.

Multi-Monitor

Full multi-monitor support with DPI-aware positioning. Cards remember their spot.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Global hotkeys that work even when TodoPin isn't focused. Fully customizable.

Lightweight & Private

~3 MB installer, minimal memory usage. All data stays local on your machine.
